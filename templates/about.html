Our Story

A journey of passion, dedication, and the love for Hualien's natural beauty

Where It All Began

Gancheng B&B was born from a simple dream: to create a sanctuary where travelers could experience the authentic beauty of Hualien while enjoying the warmth of professional hospitality. In 2015, we opened our doors in the peaceful village of Gancheng, nestled between the majestic Central Mountain Range and the Pacific Ocean.

What started as a small family-run guesthouse has grown into a beloved retreat for visitors from around the world, yet we've never lost sight of our core values: attentive service, genuine care, and a deep respect for the natural environment that surrounds us.

Sustainable Tourism

We believe in preserving Hualien's pristine environment for future generations. Our B&B uses eco-friendly practices, from solar heating to locally sourced organic breakfast ingredients.

Local Community

We work closely with local farmers, artisans, and tour guides to ensure our guests experience authentic Hualien culture while supporting the community that welcomed us.

Our Philosophy

At Gancheng B&B, we believe that true hospitality goes beyond providing a comfortable bed. It's about creating moments of connection—with nature, with local culture, and with yourself. Every detail, from the carefully selected tea in your room to the personalized recommendations for hidden trails and local eateries, is designed to enrich your Hualien experience.

Our team approaches each day with the mindset of hosts welcoming friends into our home. We take pride in our attentive, professional service that anticipates your needs while respecting your privacy and independence.

The Gancheng Experience

Wake up to the sound of birds chirping and mountains calling. Enjoy breakfast featuring fresh vegetables from nearby farms and homemade preserves. Spend your days exploring Taroko Gorge, cycling through rice paddies, or simply reading on your private balcony as clouds drift over the peaks.

In the evening, return to find your room refreshed, local tea waiting, and our staff ready with insider tips for tomorrow's adventures. This is the rhythm of life at Gancheng B&B—peaceful, intentional, and deeply connected to the landscape around us.

Looking Forward

As we continue to grow, our commitment remains unchanged: to offer a haven where travelers can slow down, breathe deeply, and experience Hualien's magic through the lens of genuine hospitality. We invite you to become part of our story and create your own memories in this beautiful corner of Taiwan.
